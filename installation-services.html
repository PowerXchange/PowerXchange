Installation Services

We offer installation services to help homeowners and businesses get started with renewable energy. Our installation team will assess your energy needs and provide a custom solution that fits your budget.

Option 1: Grid-Tied Solar PV System

This option is ideal for homeowners who want to supplement their energy usage with solar power, but still want to remain connected to the national grid. The installation includes solar panels mounted on the roof, an inverter to convert the solar energy into usable electricity, and a meter to track energy production. This system can offset up to 50% of the home's energy usage and reduce energy bills by up to 30%. The estimated cost for this option is around R80,000.

Option 2: Off-Grid Solar PV System

This option is ideal for homeowners who want to be completely self-sufficient and independent from the national grid. The installation includes solar panels mounted on the roof, an inverter to convert the solar energy into usable electricity, and a battery backup system for storing excess energy. This system can provide up to 100% of the home's energy needs, even during power outages or load shedding. The estimated cost for this option is around R150,000.
